Description

  1. Nearest Police Station
    From here you can see your nearest Police Station from your location and it’s direction.Clicking on the marker you can call the officers also.

  2. Police Station's Location
    Police station's address,direction and the location from google map.

  3. All Police Stations
    All Police Station's contact numbers are here. Clicking on an item you can send sms, share the number with others, can save the number in your mobile and can also call the officers.

  4. Traffic
    Clicking on an item you can send sms, share the number with others, can save the number in your mobile and can call the concerned traffic police officers.

  5. DB
    You can get contact numbers of Detective Branch officers here. Clicking on an item you can send sms, share the number with others, can save the number in your mobile and can also call the officers.

  6. SB
    Contact numbers of Special Branch officers are available here. Clicking on an item you can send sms, share the number with others, can save the number in your mobile and can also call the officers.

  7. Victim Support Center
    Anyone can get help for children and women calling immediately by clicking the item and can send sms, share the number with others.

  8. CMP Services
    All CMP services are described here.

  9. Idea & Development
    Those who are behind this application are here.

  10. Quick Contact
    In case of emergency, anyone can call by clicking the item and can send email or message through Facebook.

  11. How can I contact with the developers of this App?
    Press "Idea & Development". Scrolling the screen you will find the link of the developers.

  12. How to give feedback on this App?
    On the Exit-Page, you can press the Feedback button.

  13. Who developed this software?
    This android application has been developed by w3dreamers, a software volunteer team of w3xplorers. To know more about w3 dreamers, press the "Idea & Development" and click on "w3dreamers". For details you can click at the bottom of the Exit-Screen.
